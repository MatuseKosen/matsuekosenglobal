Our Institution

National Institute of Technology, Matsue College

Established in 1964, the National Institute of Technology, Matsue College (Matsue Kosen) is a leading technical college located in Matsue City, Shimane Prefecture, Japan. Our mission is to cultivate engineers who can contribute to both local and global societies.

The Global Commons, located on the 3rd floor of Building 1, serves as the on-campus hub for all international activities — from language support to exchange programme coordination.

Our Mission
